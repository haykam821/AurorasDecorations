Amethyst Lantern

Amethyst Lantern is a type of lantern which primary color is purple, like amethyst!
They emit some purple particles while placed.

In addition to the cosmetic part of this lantern, the Amethyst Lantern will cancel natural mob spawning in a 32 blocks radius in all directions around it. They will also fasten the curing of zombie villagers if near one.

Crafting Table
Amethyst Shard Lantern Amethyst Shard

Notes

  • The lantern doesn't prevent the spawning of phantoms, so do not forget to sleep!
  • The lantern doesn't prevent the spawn of mob spawners.
